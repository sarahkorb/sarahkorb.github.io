Muse is an app that transforms habit-building into a visually and emotionally immersive experience, helping you become your own muse. Through a dynamic interface, Muse seamlessly integrates with Pinterest, Spotify, Google Calendar, and other favorite apps, curating mood boards, playlists, and reminders to make each habit a personalized journey. Every few hours, Muse refreshes with custom notifications that inspire action through vivid visuals and mood-matching sounds. Users can match and share habits with friends, fostering accountability, connection, and a collective approach to self-improvement.
