MANI is a gesture-based virtual assistant that operates with a display and a rotating camera. Users can raise their hand, sign to the camera usin finger-spelling, have their requests processed, and the results displayed visually. We have encoded shortcuts for common requests to reduce the letters needed. See the dmeo and project presentation below. 
Note: the final product used a rotating camera and display, but the demo linked uses the webcamera only.
