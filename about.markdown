Hi, I’m Sarah! I’m currently a graduate student at Columbia University, where I’m studying computer science with a focus on machine learning. My academic interests lie at the intersection of AI interpretability and governance, as well as its applications. I’m excited to contribute to the responsible development and deployment of these technologies for social impact! 

I obtained my Bachelors degree from Dartmouth College in Computer Science and Economics in 2022. After graduating I moved to Washington DC where I worked at [Appian](https://appian.com/) in an infrastructure and site-reliability engineering role, using opensource platforms to make complex, microservice systems observable and accessible for developers and customers. I headed back to school in the fall of 2024 with the goal of specializing in Machine Learning.
