About me

Hi, I'm Sarah , a graduate student at Columbia University in New York City, where I study Computer Science and Machine Learning. I'm fascinated by artificial intelligence, both in its theoretical foundations and in how we can harness its advances for positive societal impact.

On campus I've contributed to research in LLM interpretability, optimization, and computational neuroscience. I also serve as a technical program facilitator for Columbia's AI Alignment Club and I am a fellow of Columbia's Effective Altruism Club. My work and interests center on AI alignment, transparency, and safety.

Before graduate school I worked as a software engineer in Washington, D.C.
