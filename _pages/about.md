Hi! I'm Sarah and I'm a Software Engineer living and working in Washington DC. I currently work in an infrastructure and site-reliability engineering role, using opensource platforms to make complex, microservice systems observable and accessible for developers and customers. 

As for my personal interests, I'm fascinated by Artifical intelligance and Machine Learning, and how we can leverage advancements in these areas for positive societal impact. I'm also interested in the building of ethical AI, AI safety, and AI policy. The tech zeitgeist aside, I think the next steps we take with AI will redefine our understanding of data, privacy, and intellectual property, as well as international trade, politics, and relations.
